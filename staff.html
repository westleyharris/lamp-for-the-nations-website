OUR STAFF

Check Us Out

Professor Zeshan Alam teaching class

The team at Lamp For The Nations brings decades of combined, hands-on ministry experience to the classroom. Our faculty have served in every role imaginable—pastors, associate pastors, youth pastors, small group leaders, administrators, College professors and global missionaries. They've ministered around the world, and now they bring those lived experiences into their teaching, integrating scripture with practical wisdom to equip and inspire their students.

Our mission as a staff is threefold:

  1. To expand your understanding of the rich knowledge found in the Bible.
  2. To ignite a passionate calling within you for ministry.
  3. To empower your success, recognizing the unique cultural challenges you face in your journey.

We're here to walk alongside you—to challenge, encourage, and prepare you for meaningful impact.
